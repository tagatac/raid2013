Registration

Registration includes admission to all RAID 2013 sessions, proceedings, and selected meals throughout the event.

Registration Type Until September 23, 2013, 11:59 EST Starting September 24, 2013
Corporate $850 $950
Academic $700 $775
Student * $450 $500
Accompanying Guest (per guest) % $150 $150
Mastercard or Visa ONLY

*-Student fees only available upon verification of student status. Please send a copy of your transcript indicating that you are a full-time student to raid-registration@lists.cs.columbia.edu

%-Accompanying Guest includes conference lunches, welcome reception, and beach BBQ.

Cancellation Policy

A refund minus a 10% handling fee will be given to registrants who are unable to attend the conference due to medical emergency. A substitute participant will be accepted at any time with written notification.

Privacy Policy

Any personal information you provide to us during registration, including your name, address, telephone number and email address, will not be released, sold, or rented to any entities. Your name and institution as specified on your registration form will be used for your conference badge.
