Visa Information

Send request for VISA invitation letter to Bruno Crispo with the following information:

  • Your name as it appears on your passport
  • Current postal mailing address
  • The name of the conference you wish to attend
  • Your Registration Confirmation Number
  • If you have any papers accepted for the conference, please provide the title

Please note visa invitation letters are issued via email by PDF and the original is mailed to your current postal address.

Hotel Information

Further options are available here. The following is a selection of the hotels closer to the venue of the conference.

Don't forget to visit the hotels' pages here for promotions code and discounts.

Hampshire Hotel Beethoven
 
Address

Beethovenstraat 43
1077 HB, Amsterdam

Info (link to the website)

Located on one of the most beautiful shopping streets in Amsterdam South, Hampshire Hotel - Beethoven, is used mainly by business people and demanding tourist. You will also find an excellent and quick connection to the centre of the city with its museums, theatres, concerts, ballet, restaurants and nightlife.

VU University Amsterdam can easily be reached by public transport.


Qbic Hotel Amsterdam WTC
 
Address

Mathijs Vermeulenpad 1
1077 XX, Amsterdam

Info (link to the website)

The Qbic Hotel in Amsterdam, is located in the World Trade Center, at 5 minutes walk from VU University Amsterdam. It is also situated very close to Amsterdam Schiphol Airport and the RAI Convention Center, and only 10 minutes by tram or taxi from the heart of Amsterdam city centre.


Hotel Novotel Amsterdam City
 
Address

Europaboulevard 10
1083 AD, Amsterdam

Info (link to the website)

Conveniently located in the centre of the southern part of Amsterdam, at only 10 minutes walk from VU University Amsterdam.

Novotel offers the charming Cote Jardin restaurant, English Pub, Coffee Corner, several boutiques and WiFi Internet throughout the hotel.
